Chicken Stew

Desciption

Everyone loves chicken stew. Here I'll show you hgow to make it

You'll need a pot and some chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • A Pot
  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook Chicken in pot
  2. Boil Potatoes for 20 minutes
  3. Add water, salt, pepper and potaotes to pot with chicken.
  4. Simmer for 45 minutes
